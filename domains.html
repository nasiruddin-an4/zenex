Questions & Answers

Domain FAQ

Have a question about domain registration, renewals, or transfers? Find quick answers below.

How do I transfer my domain to ZenexCloud?

Transferring is easy. First, log into your current registrar, unlock the domain, and copy the EPP/Auth code. Then, enter your domain name in our transfer form above. Once you enter the Auth code and complete the transfer purchase, the registry will initiate the transfer, which typically completes in 5 to 7 days. Most domain extensions automatically receive an additional year of registration upon transfer.

Is WHOIS Privacy Protection really free?

Yes, absolutely. Unlike other registrars who charge annual fees for privacy protection, ZenexCloud provides free, unlimited WHOIS privacy protection for all eligible TLDs. This keeps your personal information (name, address, email, phone number) hidden from public directories, preventing spammers and telemarketers from targeting you.

What is a TLD and which one should I choose?

TLD stands for Top-Level Domain (such as .com, .org, or .xyz). A .com is globally recognized and highly recommended for general business. Custom extensions like .xyz are very affordable (starting at $1.99/yr) and are widely used by tech startups, developers, and creative portfolios.

How long does it take for a domain to become active?

New domain registrations are processed instantly. While the domain is registered immediately, DNS propagation (the time it takes for servers worldwide to recognize your new domain settings) can take anywhere from 5 to 30 minutes. In rare cases, it may take up to 24 hours to update globally.

Can I point my domain to other hosting providers?

Yes. You have complete control over your domain's DNS zones. You can point your A, AAAA, MX, CNAME, TXT, or SRV records to any web server, hosting provider, email provider, or site builder of your choice from your simple ZenexCloud dashboard.
